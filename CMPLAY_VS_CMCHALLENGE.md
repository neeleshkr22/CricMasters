## Strategy Tips

### !cmchallenge Strategy:

4. **Pick Opponents:** Challenge users you think you can beat
5. **Bankroll Management:** Never bet more than 20% of your balance
6. **Take Breaks:** If you lose 2-3 in a row, stop challenging
